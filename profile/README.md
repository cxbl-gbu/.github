# Welcome to the Computational and Experimental biomolecular Lab (CxBL) 👋

We are based at **Gujarat Biotechnology University (GBU)**, focusing on the intersection of structural biology, machine learning, and computational bio-discovery. 

## 🔬 Our Research Focus
* **Computational Bio-Discovery:** Developing robust pipelines for drug target identification and drug discovery (e.g., targeting proteins like PBP2a and NirA).
* **Machine Learning in Biology:** Predictive modeling, including ML algorithms for pIC50 prediction.
* **Accessible Bioinformatics:** Building user-friendly Graphical User Interfaces (GUIs) to make complex molecular docking and bioinformatics tools accessible to beginners.
